Who We Are

ENERGY RADIO is a leading Rwandan radio station broadcasting from Musanze and reaching nationwide listeners through FM, digital platforms, and global streaming. We amplify voices, inspire creativity, and connect communities through entertainment, education, and impactful communication.

Our Mission

To educate, inform, entertain, empower, and positively transform communities through radio, technology, creativity, and strategic communication.

Our Vision

To become Rwanda's most innovative and socially impactful broadcasting platform, shaping the future of media and community development.
